Welcome to Rajalakshmi Engineering College

Rajalakshmi Engineering College, an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, was established in the year 1997 under the aegis of Rajalakshmi Educational Trust. The college has grown from strength to strength in the last 25 years and is currently situated in a sprawling campus of 100 acres on the Chennai-Bangalore Highway.

Our vision is to be an institution of excellence in Engineering, Technology and Management Education & Research. To provide competent and ethical professionals with a deep sense of social responsibility.
